This is about

Defence Dreamer! a website for dreamers who strives to adorn that prestogious uniform. Getting into indian armed forces has never been easy for anyone, from clearing written exams till recieving of joining letter each phase is hills and valleys. But to make this very same process a gentle walk for you Defence Dreamer comes to act with several features. Starting with well curated written examination strategies to proper plan for clearing Services Selection Board (SSB) interview everything is offered at same portal.
The strategies, plans and study materials refered on portal are all curated by the aspirants who have been successful several times in the same selection procedure. Many of the candidates who have been once a Defence Dreamer and now an Officer in the armed forces have also contributed to development of this portal.

Features

Proficient Preparation Guide

From written examinations materials to SSB cracking guide and strategy, everything is served in detailed and structured manner. Best reccomended courses for CDS, NDA, AFCAT and INET are listed for the ease of candidates

Inspirational Tales

Success stories of our defence dreamer, who also once had a dream to wear the uniform and are now living their dream. Strategies, plans and materials used by them are also described. So if you ever feel low have read and rejuvenate yourself.

Exclusive Defence Knowledge

Noteworthy defence knowledge, which is very common point of disscusion in personal interview during SSB interview. This gives one a chance to get insightful information of our Indian Armed Forces.

Defence Dreamers Chat

A public chat community for all the defence aspirants where they can discuss their doubts with fellow aspirants. It is one of the way we can support to create a heathy community of defence aspiransts and learn from each other. Dreamers chat is a way to learn togather grow togather and bear stars togather
